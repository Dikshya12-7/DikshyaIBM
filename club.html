LIONEL MESSI

A Journey Through His Legendary Club Career

Club Career Timeline

2000 – 2021

FC Barcelona

La Masia Graduate & Club Icon

Arrived as a 13-year-old prodigy and spent 21 legendary years at the club. Breaking nearly every European club record, he won 10 La Liga titles, 4 UEFA Champions League trophies, and scored a staggering 672 goals before departing in August 2021.

2021 – 2023

Paris Saint-Germain (PSG)

French Ligue 1 Champion

Spent two highly productive seasons in Paris, registering 32 goals and 34 assists in 75 total appearances. During his tenure at the Parc des Princes, he secured back-to-back Ligue 1 titles and the 2022 Trophée des Champions.

2023 – Present

Inter Miami CF

Captain & Franchise Leader

Joined the Major League Soccer (MLS) side in the summer of 2023. As captain, he quickly guided the club to its historic first-ever MLS Cup title in December 2025, and continues to put on spectacular displays in the ongoing 2026 season.
