LIONEL MESSI

The Historic Legacy of the GOAT

Historic Records

6 World Cups

The first male player in football history to feature in six separate World Cup tournaments.

Knockout Master

Holds the record for the most knockout stage assists in World Cup history.

113 G/A in a Year

The most decisive player in the 21st century in terms of producing the most goals and assists in a single calendar year.

Career Legacy & Trophies

8x Ballon d'Or Winner

Holds the absolute record for the most player of the year accolades in football history (2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2015, 2019, 2021, 2023).

International Trophies

2022 FIFA World Cup, 2021 & 2024 Copa América, and a 2008 Olympic Gold Medal.

Club Dominance

A staggering 48 total trophies won across his legendary career, including 10 La Liga titles and 4 UEFA Champions League titles with FC Barcelona, alongside silverware at Paris Saint-Germain and Inter Miami.
